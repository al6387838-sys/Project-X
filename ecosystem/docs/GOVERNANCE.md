## LifeOS Ecosystem Governance (DELTA-006)

The LifeOS Ecosystem Governance framework ensures the integrity, security, and quality of all components within the LifeOS ecosystem. It establishes clear policies, rigorous review processes, and continuous monitoring to maintain trust and compliance.

### Pillars of Governance:

*   **Review System:** All applications, plugins, and AI skills undergo automated and manual review before publication.
*   **Policies:** Comprehensive guidelines covering Code of Conduct, Data Security (GDPR, HIPAA compliance), and Content Quality standards.
*   **Certification:** Official certification programs for partners and components, ensuring adherence to LifeOS standards.
*   **Digital Signature:** Cryptographic signatures to guarantee the authenticity and integrity of all ecosystem components.
*   **Versioning:** Strict semantic versioning control with backward compatibility guarantees for all APIs and modules.
*   **Monitoring:** Real-time monitoring of security, performance, and compliance across the ecosystem.
*   **Auditing:** Continuous auditing and detailed reports for regulatory compliance and internal accountability.

### Compliance & Trust:

*   **Compliance Dashboard:** Provides an overview of compliance status for all ecosystem components.
*   **Audit Reports:** Detailed reports on security, performance, and regulatory adherence.
*   **Official Certifications:** Recognition for components and partners meeting specific quality and security benchmarks.
