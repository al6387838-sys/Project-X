# MARKETPLACE.md
## LifeOS Marketplace (DELTA-001)

The LifeOS Marketplace serves as the official distribution channel for all LifeOS-compatible applications, modules, plugins, templates, services, and integrations. It provides a curated experience for users to discover and enhance their LifeOS environment.

### Features:

*   **Extensive Catalog:** Browse and discover a wide range of offerings from LifeOS Labs and certified third-party developers.
*   **Rating and Reviews:** User-driven feedback system to ensure quality and transparency.
*   **Publishing System:** A streamlined process for developers to submit their creations.
*   **Approval Workflow:** Rigorous review and approval process to maintain high standards of security and functionality.
*   **Seamless Installation:** One-click installation for applications and modules.

### Visuals:

*   The marketplace interface is designed with a clean, modern aesthetic, featuring intuitive navigation and clear presentation of product information. (See `marketplace.html` for implementation details).
