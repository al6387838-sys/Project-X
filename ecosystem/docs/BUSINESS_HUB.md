## LifeOS Business Hub (DELTA-005)

The LifeOS Business Hub provides an integrated enterprise environment for managing all aspects of business operations, fostering seamless collaboration with suppliers, clients, and partners. It includes a dedicated B2B Marketplace for efficient procurement and sales.

### Core Modules:

*   **Enterprise Management:** Centralized control over multiple companies, departments, and teams.
*   **Supplier Management:** Onboarding, evaluation, and automated order processing for suppliers.
*   **Client Relationship Management (CRM):** Integrated CRM with comprehensive history, communication logs, and relationship analytics.
*   **Partner Network:** Tools for connecting with strategic partners and collaborating on projects.
*   **B2B Marketplace:** A dedicated platform for buying and selling B2B products and services with integrated payment solutions.
*   **Automations:** Create automated workflows between internal systems and external partners.

### Key Features:

*   **Digital Contracts:** Electronic signature and contract management.
*   **Integrated Billing:** Invoice generation, billing control, and payment processing.
*   **B2B Analytics:** Performance reports, ROI analysis, and relationship insights.
